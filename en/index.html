Conveniently located at 19 Luong Van Can, An Cuu Ward, Hue City, Fairy’s House Huế is proud to offer a warm, elegant, and exceptionally clean sanctuary for travelers.

Easy Connections Around the Ancient Capital

Fairy's House is seated at 19 Luong Van Can, An Cuu Ward — a safe, quiet street that feels peaceful yet remains incredibly close to Hue center.

Prime & Convenient Neighborhood

From 19 Luong Van Can, you are exceptionally close to everything:

  • 📍 An Dinh Palace (Cung An Định) 450m (3-min walk)
  • 📍 An Cuu Market (Chợ An Cựu) 600m (5-min walk)
  • 📍 Trang Tien Bridge (Cầu Tràng Tiền) 1.8 km (5-min drive)
  • 📍 Hue Imperial Citadel (Đại Nội Huế) 2.8 km (8-min drive)
  • 📍 Hue Railway Station (Ga Huế) 2.2 km (6-min drive)
  • 📍 Phu Bai Airport (Sân bay Phú Bài) 13 km (20-min drive)
