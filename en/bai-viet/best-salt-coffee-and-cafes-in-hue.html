A Sip of Memory: Best Salt Coffee and Beautiful Vintage Cafés in Hue

Published: June 2026 Category: Cafe and Coffee Culture Reading Time: 4-Min Read

If there is one beverage that has evolved into a contemporary cultural "heritage" of Hue, it is definitely Salt Coffee. But beyond that delightful mix of salty and creamy sweetness, Hue’s coffee culture is deeply tied to shaded green garden houses and vintage apartments where time seems to slow down. Let’s explore the top cafés you absolutely must visit when in Hue.

1. Salt Coffee Nguyen Luong Bang – The Origin of the Legend

This humble café located at 10 Nguyen Luong Bang is widely regarded as the birthplace of Hue's unique salt coffee. Over a decade ago, the couple who owned this shop experimented by topping traditional thick drip phin coffee with a light, velvety, salted foam mixture. The result was a phenomenal taste harmony.

The salinity level here is extremely delicate, with a cloud-like creaminess that is never greasy. Savoring a slow sip under their quiet garden shade, you will feel the noise of the bustling city melt away in an instant.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Primary Location: 10 Nguyen Luong Bang Street, Phu Nhuan Ward, Hue City (and branch at 142 Nguyen Hue Street, Hue City).
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 06:30 AM – 10:00 PM daily.

2. Gac Trinh – Tracing the Footsteps of Composer Trinh Cong Son

Tucked on the second floor of an ancient apartment block on Nguyen Truong To, Gac Trinh is the actual historic flat where legendary composer Trinh Cong Son lived and wrote his legendary love ballads in the 1960s. Today, it has been preserved as a beautiful vintage café and living museum.

Come here to order an authentic black coffee or cozy herbal tea, sit by the wooden shutters as dry leaves fall outside, and listen to his melancholic melodies playing from retro tape reels. This spot beautifully preserves the romantic spirit of Hue's old intelligentsia.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: 2nd Floor, 203/19 Nguyen Truong To Street, Vinh Ninh Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 07:00 AM – 09:30 PM daily.

3. Vy Da Xua – Royal Heritage Garden & Wooden Architecture

For those who wish to experience coffee or imperial tea like royal courtiers, Vy Da Xua is the ultimate destination. Built entirely in the traditional "Nha Ruong" style using dark prized woods, the property features a gorgeous landscaped setting facing the romantic Perfume River.

With soft rolling lawns, rustic stone bridges crossing water lily ponds, and birds singing in the trees, it is a magnificent location to slow down and take beautiful photographs in traditional Vietnamese attire.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: 131 Nguyen Sinh Cung Street, Vy Da Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 06:00 AM – 10:00 PM daily.

4. Chieu Café – The Cozy Attic of local Artists & Poets

Tucked right near the old Dap Da bridge, Chieu Café has stood solid for decades, serving as a peaceful refuge for generations of local writers, painters, and poets. You won't find neon lights or modern minimalist glass here—only exposed aged bricks, vintage lanterns, and wooden window benches overlooking the tranquil water.

Chieu keeps its playlist limited to classical symphonies and retro French chanson. Savoring a slow-drip coffee here while watching the lazy river flow provides an irreplaceable state of pure peace.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: Kiet 104 Kim Long Street, Kim Long Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 07:00 AM – 10:00 PM daily.
