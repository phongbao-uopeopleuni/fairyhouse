Top Attractions Located Near Fairy’s House Huế

Published: June 2026 Category: Destination Guide Reading Time: 5-Min Read

Short on vacation transit and looking to explore great sights without spending hours driving? Fairy’s House Huế, located centrally at 19 Luong Van Can, An Cuu Ward, serves as the perfect base. Here are the top historic landmarks situated exceptionally close to our homestay.

1. Hue Imperial Citadel (Dai Noi)

The Imperial Citadel is the iconic symbol of the ancient capital, bearing the golden marks of the Nguyen Dynasty. With its vast space and unique royal architecture, this is a must-experience destination, especially suitable for first-time visitors to Hue.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: 23/8 Street, Thuan Hoa Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: Summer 06:30 - 18:00; Winter 07:00 - 17:30.

2. Thien Mu Pagoda

Nestled peacefully beside the poetic Perfume River, Thien Mu Pagoda offers a tranquil spiritual space, completely detached from the noisy pace of life. This place is perfect for strolling and settling your soul in the clear early mornings or during the falling sunset.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: Kim Long Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: 08:00 - 18:00.

3. Khai Dinh Tomb

Khai Dinh Tomb is an extremely striking architectural work, representing a delicate intersection between Asian and European cultures. Every meticulously crafted porcelain detail turns this place into a perfect backdrop, great for producing artistic and unique souvenir photos.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: Chau Chu Mountain, Thuy Bang Commune, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: 07:00 - 17:30.

4. Minh Mang Tomb

Hidden amidst a quiet natural setting, Minh Mang Tomb impresses with its vast, green campus and a deeply solemn atmosphere. This is the ideal destination for travelers who love profound history and wish to explore standard royal architecture.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: Huong Tho / Long Ho Area, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: 07:00 - 17:30.

5. Tu Duc Tomb

Known as one of the most poetic and elegant mausoleum landscapes, Tu Duc Tomb is surrounded by a gentle lake and many shady trees. You can easily combine visiting this tomb with a trip to the colorful Thuy Xuan incense village.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: Thuy Ba Hamlet, Thuy Xuan Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: Summer around 06:30 - 17:00; Winter around 07:00 - 17:00.

6. Dong Ba Market

Dong Ba Market is the most bustling commercial heartbeat of Hue. This is an unmissable place if you want to buy famous specialties, souvenirs, and directly immerse yourself in the vibrant, authentic daily life of the locals.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: 2 Tran Hung Dao Street, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: Around 05:00 - 19:00.

7. Thuy Xuan Incense Village

Thuy Xuan Incense Village leaves a lasting impression with vividly colored incense bundles spread out like brilliant flowers blooming under the sun. This is an ideal check-in spot for visitors who enjoy artistic photography and want to buy traditional handmade gifts.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: Huyen Tran Cong Chua Street, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: Daytime visits; many spots are open from morning till evening.

8. Vong Canh Hill

When speaking of the most romantic sunset viewing spots in Hue, one cannot overlook Vong Canh Hill. Standing from the hilltop, you can capture the soft winding curve of the Perfume River in your view. The best time to visit is in the cool afternoon when golden sunlight stretches across the water.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: 102 Huyen Tran Cong Chua Street, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: Free to visit.

9. An Dinh Palace

An Dinh Palace is an ancient palatial structure nestled gently beside the calm An Cuu River. Its location is much closer to Fairy’s House Hue homestay than many other monuments, making it easy to take a gentle stroll to admire the beautiful royal architecture.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: 179 Phan Dinh Phung, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: Around 07:00 - 17:00 or depending on the season.

10. An Hien Garden House

If you want to seek a bit of the breath of Hue's ancient aristocracy, An Hien Garden House is the perfect destination. Boasting a traditional garden house architecture that is serene and peaceful, it is highly suitable for travelers who wish to escape the urban hustle and immerse themselves in a quiet green space.

📍 Visiting Information:

  • 📍 Address: 58 Nguyen Phuc Nguyen Street, Huong Long, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected open hours: 08:00 - 17:00.

Smart Recommended Itinerary

For visitors staying with us, we suggest a robust, relaxing itinerary:

  • Morning (8:00 AM): Start with a hot bowl of Beef Noodle Soup nearby, then take a short walk to An Dinh Palace to avoid crowds.
  • Midday (11:30 AM): Dive into Dong Ba Market's food court for affordable tapioca treats, then grab a glass of sweet ginseng tea.
  • Afternoon (3:00 PM): Visit the Imperial Citadel to enjoy golden-hour lighting across royal arches, capturing perfect photos.
  • Night (7:30 PM): Wind down with fresh fruit tea at quiet cafes back around the tranquil Luong Van Can street area.
