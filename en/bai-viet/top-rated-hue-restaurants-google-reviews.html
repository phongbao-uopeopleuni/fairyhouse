Top-Rated Hue Dining Spots According to Google Reviews

Published: June 2026 Category: Local Food Reviews Reading Time: 5-Min Read

Alongside its famed royal heritage, Hue’s vibrant street food scene is a magnet for international travelers. However, choosing the most authentic flavor among hundreds of signage banners can be tricky. To give you an objective perspective, we have curated the ultimate list of top-tier street eateries in Hue based on actual ratings and reviews by the global traveler community on Google Maps (Google Reviews).

1. Bun Bo Me Keo – Authentic & Cozy Traditional Noodle Soup

⭐ Google Maps Score: 4.4 / 5.0 (Over 1,100+ active reviews)

"Incredibly savory broth packed with the robust fragrance of lemongrass and local shrimp paste. It is served hot and spicy, matching the true Hue identity. The toppings like crab cakes, pork belly, and tender cubes of beef are sublime." - Reviewer consensus.

No giant signage, Bun Bo Me Keo is set in an ancient, quiet porch by the river of Bach Dang. They strictly preserve the historical way of serving: small vermicelli noodles, sweet bone-deep broth with a fiery kick. Guests line up and standard self-service is the charming way of life here.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: 20 Bach Dang Street, Phu Cat Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 06:00 AM – 10:00 AM (Usually sells out before 08:30 AM).

2. Com Hen Ba Cam – Legendary & Budget-Friendly Clam Rice

⭐ Google Maps Score: 4.5 / 5.0 (Over 1,300+ reviews)

"A gorgeous bowl of baby clam rice is only 15,000 VND (~$0.6 USD), yet it is filled with flavorful sautéed clams, crispy pork rinds, roasted peanuts, and finely chopped banana blossoms. Paired with a warm bowl of clam-sweet herbal broth, it is absolutely mind-blowing!" - Local guide comment.

Set near the famous Vy Da area (very close to An Cuu), Ba Cam Clam Rice is a budget legend. Starting with simple local tables, it remains packed with locals and visitors from dawn to dusk because of the fresh sweet clams and amazing spicy seasonings that immediately delight your tastebuds.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: 49 Tung Thien Vuong Street, Vy Da Ward, Hue City (Around 5 minutes driving from Fairy's House).
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 06:00 AM – 10:00 PM daily.

3. Banh Beo Me Chieu – Peaceful & Clean Family-Run Steamed Cake House

⭐ Google Maps Score: 4.8 / 5.0 (Unanimous high praise from travelers)

"An adorable hidden gem in a lovely local alleyway. The steamed cakes are hot, delicate, and made freshly upon order. The tapioca cakes are delightfully chewy, stuffed with perfectly caramelized river shrimp, and the sweet garlic-chili dipping sauce ties it all together beautifully." - Visual review notes.

There are countless steamed cake joints in Hue, but Me Chieu's stall inside An Cuu Ward (just some blocks away from Fairy's House Homestay) steals everyone's heart with absolute cleanliness and rustic hospitality. Cakes are steamed to order, ensuring they arrive on your table piping hot.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: Kiet 64 Nguyen Hue Street, Vinh Ninh Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 01:30 PM – 07:30 PM daily.

4. Salt Coffee Nguyen Luong Bang – The Golden Standard of Hue Specialty Coffee

⭐ Google Maps Score: 4.6 / 5.0 (Consistently rated as Hue's top-tier salt coffee)

"An amazing fusion of salty, dairy-rich foam topping a slow-dripping traditional Vietnamese dark filter brew. It balances bitter, salty, and sweet in a perfect mix. Definitely a highlight of my trip to Central Vietnam!" - Travel blogger note.

Salt coffee (Cà Phê Muối) has evolved into one of the symbols of modern Hue culinary culture. This local café on Nguyen Luong Bang Street serves this creamy specialty in a gorgeous shaded garden space. Standard drip filters are combined with exquisite salinity foam to warm your morning soul.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: 10 Nguyen Luong Bang Street, Phu Hoi Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 06:30 AM – 10:00 PM daily.

5. Lien Hoa Vegetarian House – Spiritual & Serene Dining

⭐ Google Maps Score: 4.4 / 5.0 (Over 2,400+ vegetarian culinary reviews)

"This place is not just for Buddhists. The vegetarian menu is surprisingly vast and delicious—offering fried rice, savory veggie hotpots, and vegan leaf-wrapped cakes in an ancient wood palace style. Highly affordable and serene." - Google Local Guide.

Hue has a rich history of vegetarian culture, and Lien Hoa stands out as the highest-reviewed spot on Google Maps. Nestled under shade trees, entering this restaurant instantly brings a peaceful, spiritual sigh with delicate incense smells and calming background meditation music.

📍 Recommended Shop, Address & Hours:

  • 📍 Address: 3 Le Hong Phong Street, Phu Nhuan Ward, Hue City.
  • ⏰ Expected hours: 07:00 AM – 09:00 PM daily.
