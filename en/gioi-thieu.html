How We Began

Our Story

Fairy's House Huế was born out of a simple dream: to plant a "green seed of peace" in a charming corner of historic Hue. We wanted to build a sanctuary where weary travelers wake up in the morning and immediately feel as warm and familiar as being in their own home.

The name "Fairy" represents purity, freshness, and the gentle comfort of sweet fairy tales. Carrying our sincere love for Hue, we transformed the house at 19 Luong Van Can, An Cuu Ward into a peaceful haven, open to everyone who appreciates the elegant beauty and warm hospitality of Thua Thien Hue people.

Design Philosophy

Elegant Lodging Style

Fairy's House is designed around the concept of a Modern Vietnamese Homestay (Cozy - Clean - Welcoming). We harmoniously combine rustic locally sourced wood furniture, soft organic fabrics, elegant warm cream tones, and lively, refreshing green plants.

We purposefully move away from the cold, extravagant designs of monumental industrial hotels to focus entirely on cozy relaxation. Each staying guest feels the genuine care in draft-free bedrooms, fresh mint-scented washrooms, a breezy terrace, and a warm kitchen that welcomes check-ins with hot ginger tea.

Our Promise

4 Golden Commitments of Fairy’s House

Consistent lodging quality down to the smallest detail is our ultimate measure of success.

1. Supreme Cleanliness

We carry out strict cleaning protocols, heat-sterilize all linens, and sanitize bathrooms using natural lemongrass-infused essences after every single checkout. All personal amenities, bedding, and fixtures are kept immaculately spotless.

2. Native Family-Like Care

We love tailor-making local Hue guide cards for you. Our hosts are delighted to share the best places for authentic tapioca cakes, scenic sunset cafes along the Huong River, or listening to traditional Hue opera, while managing motorbike rentals or Phú Bài airport pickups seamlessly.

3. Relaxing & Healing Space Experience

Every corner at Fairy's House is meticulously designed to bring peace. From the tree-shaded balcony, the quiet tea-drinking space to the cozy mattresses, everything helps you leave the hustle behind and truly enjoy the signature slow-paced lifestyle of Hue.

4. Complete Comfort Like a True Home

More than just a place to sleep, we provide full amenities so you feel it's your second home. From a cute kitchenette for free cooking and a cozy communal living room to high-speed WiFi, you will always feel as comfortable, fully equipped, and free as living in your own home.
