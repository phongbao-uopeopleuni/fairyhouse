Cozy Double Room at Fairy’s House Huế

Address: 19 Luong Van Can, Hue Bed: 1 King size double bed Capacity: Max 2 Adults Area: 22m²

Room Overview

Our Cozy Double Room at Fairy's House is a beautiful collision of classic Vietnamese architectural accents and modern conveniences. Styled in rustic, warm milk-cream tones, it welcomes daylight with soft organic timber linings that are chemical-free and extremely restful.

Every decor item has been curated from Bát Tràng ceramics and Hue rattan collections to evoke serene stillness. The double room features expansive double-pane glass windows that look out onto refreshing morning breezes near the peaceful An Cuu River canal.

Amenities & Comforts

  • Modern climate-control air conditioning
  • En-suite Private Luxury Shower
  • 100% fine thread-count organic cotton bedding
  • Premium hair dryers, towels, shampoo, and bath gel
  • High-capacity instant hot water system
  • Dedicated desk space for remote laptops
  • Private reading lamps and handy wall charging adapters
  • Complimentary high-speed fiber internet connection

Check-in & Stay Policies

• Check-in hours: From 14:00 PM onwards.
• Check-out hours: Before 12:00 PM noon the following day.
• Air Quality: Fairy's House enforces a strict indoor NON-SMOKING rule to keep premium cotton textiles safe, fresh, and hypoallergenic. Designated smoking zones are prepared in the gardens.
